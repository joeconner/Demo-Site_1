The Experience

From ground crew to astronaut.

Every Aphelion journey is a full week — because the launch is only the beginning. Here's how your mission unfolds.

01

Astronaut Training

Four days of centrifuge sessions, zero-G flights, and mission simulations at our desert campus.

02

Launch Day

Suit up, strap in, and feel three-and-a-half G's press you into your seat as the engines light.

03

The Overview

Unbuckle in weightlessness and float to the window for the view astronauts call life-changing.

04

Homecoming

A guided re-entry, a soft landing, and a celebration with the crew who trained alongside you.

Before You Fly

Mission questions.

No. Our suborbital flights are designed for healthy adults across a wide range of ages and fitness levels. You'll complete a medical screening and training program that prepares your body for the G-forces and weightlessness. Most of our astronauts are everyday people, not professional pilots.

Suborbital missions include two days of training; orbital missions include four days; and lunar missions require roughly two weeks. Training covers centrifuge sessions, zero-G parabolic flights, emergency procedures, and full mission rehearsals at our desert campus.

Yes. Your reservation deposit is fully refundable up until you begin your training program. After that, it converts to a mission credit that can be transferred to a future launch window if needed.

Across 41 crewed launches we've maintained a 100% safe-return record. Every vehicle is human-rated, flight-tested extensively before carrying passengers, and equipped with full launch-abort systems. Safety is the single non-negotiable of every mission.
